SUIT UP

"Keep calm and ace your Interview"

This interview gives you the feel of attending the real deal without the fear of getting the job. Get through 2 rounds of elimination to face the interviewers and bag the prize. May the candidate win!

Rules:

Round 1: Aptitude and Reasoning test
  1. One mark will be awarded for each right answer.
  2. No negative marks for wrong answer.
  3. For working out, sheets will not be provided. Candidates can make use of their own notepads.
  4. Foreign materials like calculators and books will not be allowed inside the hall.
  5. Mobile phones will be allowed but using search engines like Google on it during the competition will lead to direct disqualification.
  6. After evaluation, the top 10 candidates will be invited to the group discussion via phone(Text/Call)
Round 2: Group Discussion
  1. The topic will be given on spot.
  2. Marks will be reduced for repetition of same points.
  3. Marks will be reduced for copying the points made by the other candidates.
  4. The Top 5 candidates will be invited for the one on one interview.
Round 3: Mock Interview
  1. The finalists are expected to be professionally dressed.
  2. Points will be reduced for wearing casuals and/or slippers.
  3. The finalists should have a hard copy of their resume.
  4. Additional points will be provided if the finalists come with a file containing their certificates.
  5. The best performer will be awarded an exciting cash prize.
  6. The decision of the judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.
