IPL AUCTION

"It’s time to be the next Srini Mama!"

Be the boss! Build your team of power house by having players like AB and VK play for your team with the help of your management skills and presence of mind.

Rules:

Preliminary Round:

Preliminary round is a written test which involves 30 questions from IPL and other international formats of cricket.

  1. The participants are not allowed to use mobile phones during the time of the test.
  2. A team can consist from a minimum of 1 to a maximum of 4 members.
  3. One mark will be awarded for every question.
  4. No negative marks for wrong answers.
  5. The duration of the test will be 30 minutes.
  6. Malpractice of any kind will not be encouraged and will result to disqualification.
  7. After evaluation, the top 5 teams will be invited to the finals via phone (Text/Call).
Finals:

Final round is an auction round similar to IPL auction with a pool of 90 players. Every player will be given a star quotient for understanding the value of players. The star quotient will be displayed before the auction. A team will be allotted 25 crore initially for bidding.

  1. Every player will be displayed at auction for 20 seconds.
  2. A team can re-auction only 3 unsold players after the first round of auction.
  3. Star quotient of all the players will be displayed only once before the auction.
  4. The auctioneer’s decision is the final decision.
  5. Every team is supposed to pick 5-6 batsmen, 3-4 all-rounders, 1+ wicketkeeper and 4-6 bowlers.
  6. The players will be up for auction under no sets.
  7. Every team is subjected to have a maximum of 6 foreign players.
  8. If the participant’s team points are equal, a special tie breaker round will be conducted.
  9. The points for each team will be allotted based on the cumulative star quotient of the particular team.
  10. If the tie breaker proves to be equal as well, then the amount of money saved/spent by the participants will be taken into consideration.
