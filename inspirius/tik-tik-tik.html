TIK TIK TIK

"During a quiz, people look up for information, down in desperation, left and right for verification"

Bring your squad to battle it out against other competitive teams to win the technical quiz. Your squad will be put through the written test first. If you qualify, you shall become a part of the buzzer round. The squad, who makes it through and arises as the winner, will conquer all.

Rules:

Preliminary Round:

Preliminary round is a written test which involves questions from basic physics and mathematics where your conceptual thinking and problem solving ability will be tested.

  1. A team can consist of a minimum of 1 and a maximum of 4 members.
  2. The team members can be from different engineering colleges and from different streams of engineering.
  3. One mark will be awarded for each right answer.
  4. No negative marks for wrong answer.
  5. For working out, sheets will not be provided. Participants can make use of their own notepads.
  6. Foreign materials like calculators and books will not be allowed inside the hall.
  7. Mobile phones wonâ€™t be allowed to use during the time of play.
  8. After evaluation, the top 5 teams will be invited to the finals via phone (Text/Call).
Finals:

One of the organizers will read out the questions. The team which presses the buzzer first and gives the right answer will be awarded points.

  1. The finalists are expected to be on time for the competition.
  2. Marks will be reduced if a team discusses within itself after pressing the buzzer.
  3. Buzzer should not be pressed until a question is fully finished. If a team fails to do so, marks will be reduced even if the answer is right.
  4. After 10 questions, the top 2 teams on the points table will be declared as the winner and the runner-up respectively.
  5. In case of close calls, the organizer might conduct a tie breaker.
  6. 6. The decision of the organizers will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.
