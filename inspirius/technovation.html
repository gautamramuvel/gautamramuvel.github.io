TECHNOVATION

"The people who are crazy enough to think they can change the world are the ones who do" - Steve Jobs

Troubleshoot the problems in our society with your technical innovation. We provide you the platform to help change the world, one prototype at a time. Get a chance to compete with other innovators and prove yourself.

Project Domains:

  • Internet of Things
  • Robotics & Automation
  • Healthcare Application
  • Machine Learning & Deep Learning
  • Computer Vision & Image Processing
  • Network and Security Systems
  • Smart City
  • Unmanned Vehicles
  • Green Technology

Any interesting or innovative ideas (irrespective of department) that do not fall under the above mentioned categories are also welcomed.

Rules:

  1. A team can contain a minimum of 1 and a maximum of 4 members.
  2. The team participants can be from different engineering colleges and from different streams of engineering.
  3. Teams has to send their abstract(not exceeding 500 words) to the mail technovation2018@gmail.com with the subject as "Project Name".
    Example: Sub: "Driverless car"
  4. The body of the mail should contain the team details such as:
    • Participant's Name
    • Department
    • College
    • Phone Number
    • Email Address
  5. The jury evaluates your idea and the top 20 teams will be invited for “Technovation” via mail.
  6. The teams must bring their working prototype on the day of the competition.
  7. Points will be awarded based on the way the team explains their project and also for the application and the future scope of the project.
  8. The top 3 teams will have exciting cash prizes.
  9. The decision of the judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.
