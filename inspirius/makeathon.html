MAKE-A-THON v1.0

"We all have the same 24 hours... why not MAKE full use of it?"

Make-a-thon v1.0 is a unique 24-hour competition where the brightest minds will be challenged to solve a problem statement by implementing their neoteric ideas. It is the perfect place for night owls to hang out with your posse and work for 24 hours without a wink of sleep. We provide you the best opportunity to realize your full potential and test your limits as you race against time to successfully implement your solution.

Suggested Domains:

  • Smart Grid
  • Smart Transportation
  • Smart City
  • Green Energy
  • Augmented Reality(AR)
  • Artificial Intelligence(AI)
  • Cloud Computing
  • Internet of Things(IoT)
  • Industrial Internet of things(IIoT)
  • Virtual Reality(VR)

Suggested Problem Statements:

A working prototype to solve the given problem statement is enough. Any innovative ideas outside the above mentioned statements are also welcomed.
  1. Traffic Management System

    2. With ever increasing traffic in the country, control the traffic based on the density of the traffic using the emerging technologies.

  2. Aquarium Assistant

    3. Control your fish tank, feed your fish, and more from anywhere in the world. Forgetting to feed your fish everyday? No problem! Control your fish tank with a simple low cost and power usage hardware supporting voice command.

  3. Smart Garbage System

    4. Garbage truck use to go around the town to collect solid waste twice a day. Although this system was thorough it was very inefficient. Any innovative idea to make the garbage system smart and efficient is expected from the team.

  4. Security Surveillance System (IoT)

    5. In this theme participants are advised to submit their innovative ideas based on Internet of Thing concepts to improve the security system surveillance. For example, take the use case of object recognition in the context of home & office security and surveillance.

  5. Self Defense Systems for Women

    6. A solution to protect women from molestation and harassment and also sends information about the victim automatically to the nearby police department.

  6. Smart Cycle (Chassis can be used for prototype instead of actual cycles)

    7. The main motto of this statement is to make our normal cycle a running gym equipment. It should have a display which displays the speed of the cycle, time elapsed and also find the calories lost, average speed and give cyclists an opportunity to set daily goals and finally they will see themselves fulfilling those goals.

  7. Robotic Assistive Device for disabled persons

    8. Due to unfortunate events/accidents or by birth certain person become divyang. A solution is to be proposed for development of a robotics assistive device which will complement the function not available to a divyang person.

Rules:

  1. A team can consist of a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 4 members.
  2. The team members can be from different engineering colleges and from different streams of engineering.
  3. The participants should bring their College Identity card.
  4. Any teams not sticking to the format of "ABSTRACT TEMPLATE" will not be considered.
  5. Teams are expected to have the hard copy of their abstract (in “ABSTRACT TEMPLATE” format) in a file on the day of the competition.
  6. The team is expected to bring all the necessary components as we will not be providing you with any component. But internet connection will be provided still the participating teams are expected to have their own internet connection as backup.
  7. The team has to start their project from scratch on the day of the competition.
  8. Points will be awarded for the speed and robustness of the implementation.
  9. No restrictions on dress code (Formal/Casual) but any participants found misbehaving or violating the rules and regulations will be disqualified along with their team.
  10. The decision of the judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.

Frequently Asked Questions

Can participants from different college and different departments form a team?

Yes, you can.

Can participants from different departments form a team?

Yes, you can.

Do we need to bring our own components?

We will provide you the components that you require. You can bring your own components if you want.

What are the things that we need to bring?
  • Laptop
  • Laptop Charger
  • Internet Connection
  • Abstract
  • Pendrive (If necessary)
  • Mouth Freshener
How do we know that we are shortlisted?

Shortlisted teams will be intimated through mail.

Will the management provide refreshment and food?

We will provide you refreshment, lunch (1st day), dinner (1st day) and breakfast (2nd day).

Can we submit a project that has already been implemented?

Yes, you can. But the team should start their project from the scratch on the day of the competition. Implementing the hardware connections and codes before the day of the competition and just presenting the output on the day of the event will lead to direct disqualification.

Can we have our guardian inside the competition zone?

No individuals other than the participants will be allowed inside the competition zone.

Can we pay On-spot?

Yes, you can.

What if we damage the components or burn them?

You have to pay for the damaged component (in case the components are provided by the management).

Can we get spare components in case we damage the existing one?

You have to pay the management in case you want a new component on the day of the competition.

Can we take our project after the competition?

No, the components that are provided by the management should be submitted to the organizers at the end of the competition.

Will we have participation certificate?

Except winning teams, all the other teams will be provided with the participation certificates. (Each member will be awarded a participation certificate)

Do we have Cash prize?

Top 3 teams will be provided winning certificate with exciting cash prizes.

Should we follow any dress code?

No, both formals and casuals will be allowed inside the competition zone.
