THE ARTICULATOR

"Ever wanted a soapbox to voice your opinion? Here’s your chance!"

He, who makes his ideas known to the world, is celebrated unlike the person who only invents in his closed room. A fine example? - Steve Jobs

Who knows...you could be the next celebrated orator. So don’t miss out this opportunity to present your ideas in front of academicians.

Call for Papers

  • Nanotechnology
  • Robotics & Artificial Intelligence Neuro-Fuzzy Control
  • Internet Of Things
  • Signal & Image Processing
  • Embedded Systems
  • Virtual Instrumentation
  • Hybrid Electric Vehicles
  • Non-conventional energy sources
  • Biomedical Technology
  • Automation in automobiles
  • PLC & SCADA

Any interesting or innovative ideas (irrespective of department) that do not fall under the above mentioned categories are also welcomed.

Rules:

  1. A team can have a minimum of 1 and maximum of 3 authors.
  2. The abstract must contain the names of the team members, college and contact details (email ID and mobile number).
  3. The abstract must not exceed 500 words, and must be sent to thearticulator2018@gmail.com on or before 13-08-2018(11:59PM)
  4. The subject of the mail must contain the topic that has been chosen by the author.
    Example: Sub: "Bio Medical Instrumentation"
  5. Accepted entries will be intimated by mail, following which the paper (not exceeding more than 8 pages) is to be sent.
  6. The paper must be sent in IEEE format on or before 17-08-2018 (11:59 PM).
  7. The articulator team might get in contact with the respective authors to make some changes in the paper if necessary.
  8. Selected teams must bring 2 hardcopies of their paper along with a softcopy (.doc format) of the same as well as the PowerPoint presentation (.ppt format) on a pen drive.
  9. For each paper 10 minutes will be allocated; 8 minutes for presenting and 2 minutes for question and answer session.
  10. The decision of the judges will be final and no arguments or appeal will be entertained.
